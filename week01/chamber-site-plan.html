WDD 230: Vancouver Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

Vancouver Chamber of Commerce. This name represents the city in which the chamber operates.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Vancouver Chamber of Commerce website is to assist with the orgaization's mandate of acting as a regional economic catalyst, leadership convener and community champion. The Chamber promotes business through member services, economic and community development, and public policy that balances economic prosperity.

Scenarios

What volume of imports/exports is being conducted through the Port of Vancouver?

Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?

When and what are the up-coming networking events that the chamber will be holding?
